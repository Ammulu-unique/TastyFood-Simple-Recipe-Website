Gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya
  • Maida
  • Sugar
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom

Process

  1. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water with cardamom.
  2. Grate khoya and mix with maida and baking soda.
  3. Add milk slowly and knead into a soft dough.
  4. Divide dough and roll into smooth balls.
  5. Heat ghee or oil on low flame.
  6. Fry the balls slowly until golden brown.
  7. Remove and drain excess oil.
  8. Soak fried balls in warm sugar syrup.

  10. Serve warm or chilled.

Motichoor laddu

Ingredients

  • Besan
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee
  • Cardamom
  • FoodColor
  • Cashews
  • Raisins

Process

  1. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, and cardamom.
  2. Make a smooth batter using besan and water.
  3. Add food color to the batter.
  4. Heat ghee in a pan on medium flame.
  5. Pour batter through a perforated ladle into hot ghee.
  6. Fry the tiny boondi until just cooked.
  7. Remove boondi and soak in warm sugar syrup.
  8. Add fried cashews and raisins.
  9. Mix gently until syrup absorbs.
  10. Shape into round laddus while warm.
